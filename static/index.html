Inside the PDF
01 — 07 / 07

Section by section.

Seven parts, delivered at the close of the assessment

Whether NIS2 applies to your company — directly (Annex I/II sectors) or indirectly via supply chain pressure from your clients. Includes confidence level and legal basis.
A score from 0–100 based on your answers, with overall risk level (Critical / High / Medium / Low), executive headline, and a note on what your company already has going for it.
A table covering all 10 requirements of Article 21(2) NIS2. For each: status (missing/partial/met), risk level, what to do, estimated effort, and cost range.
The 3 most important actions ranked by risk impact, with specific steps, article references, and deadlines. This is what to do first.
PASS or FAIL verdict from a simulated NIS2 inspector. Lists each critical failure with specific article citations, and what you'd need to fix before re-inspection.
Personalised attack scenarios based on YOUR specific gaps — not generic threats. Shows how an attacker would exploit your company's weaknesses and how to fix each vector.
Draft security policies for your critical gaps — a starting point you can give to a lawyer or IT contractor. Not final legal documents, but saves 2–3 hours of consultant time.
